BMW M5

The BMW M5 is a high-performance sports sedan that combines luxury and power. Known for its dynamic driving experience, the M5 is a benchmark in the world of performance cars.

Key Specifications:

  • Engine: 4.4L V8 Twin-Turbo
  • Horsepower: 600 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 3.2 seconds
  • Top Speed: 155 mph (electronically limited)
  • Drivetrain: All-wheel drive

With a perfect balance of speed, handling, and luxury, the BMW M5 stands out as an iconic sports sedan in the automotive world.
